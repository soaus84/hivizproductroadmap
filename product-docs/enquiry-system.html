✦ Enquiry Summary — What We Learned
AI Generated · 9 of 12 responses
This enquiry confirms that the spotter management gap identified in CI-042 is real, widespread, and known to supervisors. Across 4 sites and 9 responses, only 2 supervisors report the spotter assurance check is consistently in place. The root issue is not awareness — supervisors understand the risk — it is the absence of a formal mechanism for maintaining the spotter role once assigned. No site has a documented spotter handover process. Work as Done responses describe a consistent informal pattern: assignment at the brief, no system to maintain it during the task, no handover when the spotter needs to leave. Supervisors are relying on informal awareness rather than a managed control.
Recommended Actions from Enquiry
Develop and embed a spotter handover protocol into the PTW and pre-task brief template — applicable across all sites in scope
Update the STMP at all 4 source sites to define spotter positioning requirements and handover responsibilities explicitly
Generate a follow-up toolbox talk using this enquiry summary as the primary content — 9 supervisor voices informing a crew conversation is a stronger signal than an AI-drafted insight alone
